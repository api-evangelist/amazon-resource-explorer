Amazon Route 53 Global Resolver now enables you to share DNS views with other AWS accounts using AWS Resource Access Manager (AWS RAM). Consumer accounts can associate their own Route 53 private hosted zones with a shared DNS view, making their records resolvable through the owner's global resolver in every AWS Region where it runs—without transferring ownership of the hosted zone or the DNS view. With DNS view sharing, teams can continue to own and manage their private hosted zones while making them resolvable through a centralized global resolver.
