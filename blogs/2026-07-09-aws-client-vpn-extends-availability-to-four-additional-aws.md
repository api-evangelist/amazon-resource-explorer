AWS Client VPN is now available in four new regions: Canada West (Calgary), Mexico (Central) and two in Asia Pacific - New Zealand and Taipei. This fully managed service enables customers to securely connect their remote workforce to resources in AWS or on-premises networks. AWS Client VPN eliminates the need for hardware VPN appliances and complex operational management through its pay-as-you-go model.
