Starting today, Amazon Elastic Compute Cloud (Amazon EC2) G7 instances powered by NVIDIA RTX PRO 4500 Blackwell Server Edition GPUs are now available in US East (N. Virginia) Region. G7 instances deliver up to 4.6x AI inference performance and up to 2.1 graphics performance compared to G6 instances.
