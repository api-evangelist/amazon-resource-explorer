OpenAI GPT, OpenAI GPT OSS, and NVIDIA Nemotron models are now FedRAMP High and Department of Defense Cloud Computing Security Requirements Guide (DoD CC SRG) Impact Level (IL) 4 and 5 approved within Amazon Bedrock in the AWS GovCloud (US) Regions. Federal agencies, public sector organizations, and other enterprises with FedRAMP High and DoD CC SRG IL-4/5 compliance requirements can now use these models on Amazon Bedrock to build and scale generative AI applications with confidence that they meet the security and compliance standards required for government workloads.
