AWS Outposts now provides self-service capabilities for configuration, quoting, ordering, subscription management, renewal, and decommissioning directly from the AWS Management Console, CLI, and API. Previously, customers relied on AWS teams for managing their Outposts lifecycle, from evaluation through end of term. A new configuration and quoting tool generates real-time cost estimates across payment options and term lengths, and proactively surfaces account and regional constraints before order submission.
